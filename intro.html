Over the last few years, the world of streaming has exploded.
As they compete for users' attention, video streaming platforms have gotten increasingly competitive.

One of the most serious challenges to these services' viability is 'decision fatigue', in which the act of selecting something to watch can overwhelm and discourage viewers if they are unable to discover something fascinating in a reasonable amount of time.

As a result of this problem, streaming platforms have used a variety of ways to develop their user interfaces.
This guide will walk you through three different strategies used by Netflix, Prime Video, YouTube and Twitch.
